Hawaii toast

Description

A delicous hawaii toast

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Pineapple
  • Cheese
  • Ham

Steps

  1. Put the Ham on the toast
  2. Now put the pineapple slices on the ham
  3. Now put the cheese slices on the pineapple
  4. Finally put the hawaii toast in the oven for a few minutes until the ham is nice and crisp
  5. Enjoy
